Master Chefs

Simai Sarai is a well known chef in Kenya, Tanzania, South Africa and East Africa. Simai was born in Cape Town, South Africa. She has started working in restaurants when she was barely 18 and has more than 15 years of experience. At the age of 22 Simai became a Chef and moved to Egypt, where she curated more than 20 restaurants emphasizing local products and East African delicacies. For the last several years in Egypt she was Chef in the legendary Culina, which received the Michelin recommendation under Simai's guidance. In Kenya, she received GQ Super Woman of the Year Award and other significant awards. Alena’s goal as a chef is to create very honest, sensual and graceful gastronomy, which brings deep, yet delicate flavor to every guest.

Quality Food

Our cuisine is tailored to excite the sense of sight and taste without compromising the natural goodness of the ingredients. The menu is based on local and worldwide grown vegetables combined with stunning selection of delicacies, fish, seafood and meat cooked on fire. Some of the courses are inspired by our Chef Simai Sarai’s heartwarming childhood memories and have delicate Kenyan touch. Chef recommends you to try carrot with passion fruit as appetizers, cauliflower risotto with scallops and caviar as a main dish, and apple with mint and coconut as a dessert
